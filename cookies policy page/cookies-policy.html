EduBridge Cookies Policy

Effective Date: 20-09-2024

This Cookies Policy explains how EduBridge ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or use our educational platform for learning business. By accessing or using EduBridge, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login details, language preferences, and display settings) over a period of time, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time you visit.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of our platform. Without them, certain features like secure login and course access cannot work properly. Essential cookies cannot be disabled.

b. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use our platform, such as which pages you visit and any errors you encounter. The data collected is anonymous and helps us improve the performance and user experience of EduBridge

Examples:

  • Google Analytics
  • Internal analytics tools

c. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies enable the platform to remember your preferences, such as your language settings or previous course selections. These cookies help enhance your experience by personalizing content for you.

d. Targeting and Advertising Cookies

We use these cookies to show you relevant content and ads based on your browsing habits. These cookies also help limit the number of times you see the same advertisement and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

3. Third-Party Cookies

EduBridge may also use cookies from third-party services, such as:

  • Payment processing services
  • Advertising platforms
  • Social media networks (if you choose to link your accounts)
These third-party cookies are subject to the privacy and cookies policies of those third parties.

4. How We Use Cookies

EduBridge uses cookies to:

  • Authenticate your login and secure your account
  • Personalize your experience by remembering your preferences and course history
  • Analyze how users interact with the platform to improve functionality and performance
  • Provide targeted advertisements and promotions based on your interests
  • Track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

5. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the option to control and manage cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies after each browsing session
  • Restrict or disable specific types of cookies

Please note that disabling essential or functional cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of EduBridge, such as logging into your account or accessing courses.

6. How to Manage Cookies

Each browser provides different methods for managing cookies. You can typically find cookie management options in your browser's "Settings" or "Preferences" menu. Below are links to the cookie management settings for popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

7. Changes to This Cookies Policy

EduBridge may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about our use of cookies.

8. Contact Us
