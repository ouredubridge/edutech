EduBridge Cookies Policy

Effective Date: 20-09-2024

This Cookies Policy explains how EduBridge ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit or use our educational platform for learning business. By accessing or using EduBridge, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of personal information to provide and improve our services

a. Personal Identification Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information (if applicable)

b. Account Information

  • Username and password
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Course preferences and history

c. Usage Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages viewed and time spent on the platform
  • Device and operating system

d. Third-Party Data

We may receive information from third-party services such as social media platforms if you choose to link them to your account

2. How We Use Your Information

EduBridge uses your personal information to:

  • Provide access to courses and resources
  • Facilitate payments and course registration
  • Send you notifications regarding course updates, promotions, and customer support
  • Improve user experience by personalizing content and recommendations
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance platform features
  • Comply with legal requirements

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners for the following purposes

  • Payment processing
  • Email and communication services
  • Marketing (with your consent)
  • Legal and regulatory compliance
We ensure that our partners adhere to the same strict confidentiality and security practices as EduBridge.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

EduBridge uses cookies and similar technologies to track your activity on our platform. Cookies help us:

  • Remember your login details
  • Customize your experience
  • Analyze site traffic and performance
You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to fully utilize our services.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. This includes encryption, firewalls, and secure servers. However, no method of online data transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. If you wish to delete your account, you may contact us, and we will process your request in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Your Rights

As an EduBridge user, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information.
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information.
  • Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [Insert Contact Information].

8. Children's Privacy

EduBridge is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will delete the information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

EduBridge reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
